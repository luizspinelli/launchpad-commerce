# 👥 User Personas — LaunchPad Commerce

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** March 11, 2026  
**Purpose:** Understand primary & secondary users, their needs, behaviors, and pain points

---

## Overview

LaunchPad serves **2 primary user types:**
1. **Creators** (sellers) — People who want to sell digital products online
2. **Customers** (buyers) — People looking to buy digital products easily

This document focuses on **detailed personas** for each type.

---

## 🎯 Persona 1: Creator — "Sarah, The Online Educator"

### Demographics
- **Age:** 32
- **Location:** São Paulo, Brazil
- **Education:** Master's degree (Education)
- **Income:** R$3,000-5,000/month (day job) + looking to supplement with courses

### Background
Sarah is a full-time teacher but wants to build an online business teaching Python programming to beginners. She has 2,000 followers on Twitter/LinkedIn but no way to monetize yet. She's frustrated with Skillshare's revenue split (take 50%) and Udemy's complexity.

### Tech Comfort
- **Level:** Medium (knows WordPress, some coding)
- **Tools used:** Google Docs, Canva, Twitter, Teachable (tried, too expensive)
- **Pain point:** Setup complexity, too many steps, too much technical jargon

### Goals
1. Launch online course within 1 month
2. Make R$500-1,000/month from course sales
3. Build email list of students
4. Spend minimal time on technical setup (wants to focus on content)

### Pain Points
- Feels intimidated by Shopify (too many features for simple course)
- Can't afford Teachable's €29-99/month plans
- Tried WordPress + WooCommerce, too complex
- Existing audience on Twitter but no way to sell
- Doesn't want to manage student logins & account systems (MVP focus)

### Motivation
- Wants to prove course viability before investing time/money
- Looking for "lean MVP" tool (no-code, quick setup)
- Wants beautiful, professional-looking landing page (doesn't want to hire designer)
- Dreams of passive income from digital products

### Needs (from LaunchPad)
✅ **Quick setup** (<1 hour)  
✅ **Beautiful landing page** (out-of-the-box template)  
✅ **Simple product management** (add course, set price, upload)  
✅ **Automatic email confirmations** (build trust with customers)  
✅ **Stripe integration** (already has account)  
✅ **Analytics** (see who bought, revenue tracking)  
✅ **Free/cheap** (R$0-50/month)  
✅ **Mobile responsive** (customers buy on phones)  

### Behaviors
- Checks Twitter/email 10+ times/day
- Works on side projects evenings & weekends
- Prefers written tutorials & YouTube over docs
- Buys courses online monthly (researches solutions)
- Uses Figma for design (basic templates)

### Objections to Address
- *"Will my product be secure?"* → Stripe handles all security
- *"How hard is setup?"* → Shows quick setup guide (5 min video)
- *"Can I customize the design?"* → Easy color/text changes via config
- *"What if Stripe goes down?"* → Vercel + Stripe = 99.9% uptime
- *"Do I own my customer list?"* → Yes, all data is yours

### Quote
> "I just want to put my course online and sell it. I don't want to spend weeks figuring out technical stuff. LaunchPad makes me look professional without the headache."

## 🎯 Persona 2: Creator — "Marcus, The Freelance Designer"

### Demographics
- **Age:** 28
- **Location:** Remote (originally Rio de Janeiro)
- **Education:** Self-taught designer
- **Income:** €2,000-4,000/month (freelance projects)

### Background
Marcus is a talented UI/UX designer who creates reusable design templates (Figma files, UI kits, component libraries). He sells on Gumroad but wants his own branded store to reduce platform dependency.

### Tech Comfort
- **Level:** Medium-High (designer, some coding knowledge)
- **Tools used:** Figma, Gumroad, Stripe, Discord
- **Pain point:** Platform dependency (Gumroad takes 10% fee), no control over branding

### Goals
1. Build own "Marcus Design Studio" branded store
2. Sell €50-500 templates/kits monthly
3. Email marketing to repeat customers
4. Create professional storefront (currently looks amateur on Gumroad)

### Pain Points
- Gumroad takes 10% commission (€500 = lose €50)
- Can't customize branding (Gumroad logo everywhere)
- Limited customer analytics (who bought? when? how often?)
- Wants email list but Gumroad integration is weak
- No way to offer subscriptions (customers always want access to latest versions)

### Motivation
- Wants independence from platforms
- Sees own storefront as portfolio piece (impress clients)
- Understands value of branding (designer mindset)
- Dreams of recurring revenue (subscriptions = future)

### Needs (from LaunchPad)
✅ **Custom domain** (marcus.design, not launchpad.spinelli.dev)  
✅ **Beautiful, designer-friendly templates**  
✅ **Customizable color schemes & typography**  
✅ **Product gallery** (showcase multiple designs)  
✅ **Low fees** (wants to keep 98-99%)  
✅ **Customer management** (email list, repeat buyers)  
✅ **Analytics** (which templates sell best? who's buying?)  
✅ **Future: subscriptions** (recurring access to templates)  

### Behaviors
- Active on Twitter (shares design tips)
- Uses Gumroad, Stripe, Slack daily
- Values design quality (visual first)
- Researches SaaS tools thoroughly before buying
- Prefers platforms over DIY solutions

### Objections to Address
- *"Can I use my own domain?"* → Yes (future phase, already in roadmap)
- *"How much does it cost?"* → Currently free, later €9/month Pro plan
- *"Can I design my own look?"* → Full Tailwind/CSS customization
- *"Will my customers be safe paying?"* → Stripe PCI Level 1, safest option

### Quote
> "I need a storefront that makes my work look as good as the designs I create. LaunchPad's minimalist design pairs perfectly with my templates, and I keep 100% of revenue."

## 🎯 Persona 3: Creator — "Ana, The Solopreneur Coach"

### Demographics
- **Age:** 45
- **Location:** São Paulo
- **Education:** MBA + 15 years business experience
- **Income:** €5,000-10,000/month (1-on-1 coaching)

### Background
Ana is a business coach offering 1-on-1 mentoring but wants to create scalable digital products (mini-courses, workbooks, templates). She has a strong email list (5,000 subscribers) and wants to test product-market fit before building a full course platform.

### Tech Comfort
- **Level:** Low (non-technical, uses email + Zoom)
- **Tools used:** Gmail, Zoom, Notion, Mailchimp
- **Pain point:** Overwhelmed by tech options, wants something "dead simple"

### Goals
1. Sell digital workbooks/mini-courses within 2 months
2. Generate €1,000-5,000/month from digital products
3. Reduce 1-on-1 dependency (scaling challenge)
4. Build Authority (published work = credibility)

### Pain Points
- Doesn't understand (or want to learn) WordPress/Shopify
- Afraid of over-complicated tech (too many options, analysis paralysis)
- Perfectionist (wants professional look without hiring designer)
- Worried about customer data security (compliance, GDPR)
- Already stretched thin (doesn't want to learn new platforms)

### Motivation
- Wants passive income (tired of trading time for money)
- Needs social proof (customers buying = validation)
- Dreams of being recognized expert (published products = authority)
- Age-related: Wants to build sellable asset (exit eventually)

### Needs (from LaunchPad)
✅ **Dead simple setup** (no coding, no customization needed)  
✅ **Professional, premium design** (Ana's brand = high-end)  
✅ **Secure payment** (GDPR compliant, customer data safe)  
✅ **Email integration** (send confirmation + upsells)  
✅ **No ongoing maintenance** (pay & forget)  
✅ **Customer support** (real humans, not docs)  
✅ **Free initially** (test before paying)  
✅ **Beautiful templates** (no design work needed)  

### Behaviors
- Reads business books & takes courses monthly
- Distrusts overly-complex tech solutions
- Values time over money (willing to pay for simplicity)
- Asks friends for recommendations (word-of-mouth trust)
- Prefers phone calls to email support

### Objections to Address
- *"Is it secure?"* → Stripe PCI Level 1 certified, all compliance covered
- *"What if something breaks?"* → Hosted on Vercel (99.9% uptime), not your problem
- *"Can you help me set it up?"* → Quick onboarding call, all easy
- *"Is it professional looking?"* → Premium design included, no customization needed
- *"What about my customer data?"* → Your data stays yours, GDPR compliant

### Quote
> "I don't want to think about tech. I want to upload my course, share a link, and have customers pay. LaunchPad just works, and it looks beautiful."

## 🎯 Persona 4: Customer — "João, The Lifelong Learner"

### Demographics
- **Age:** 35
- **Location:** São Paulo
- **Education:** Engineering degree
- **Income:** €3,000-5,000/month (salaried job)

### Background
João is curious, always learning new skills (AWS, machine learning, web design). He buys courses online monthly (~€30-100). He's researching cloud architecture certifications and looking for affordable, quality courses from independent instructors.

### Tech Comfort
- **Level:** High (software engineer background)
- **Tools used:** AWS, GitHub, VS Code, Slack

### Goals
1. Learn AWS architecture (preparation for certification)
2. Find quality course (not bloated like Udemy courses)
3. Pay fair price (doesn't want to subsidize instructor's Mercedes)
4. Get certificate of completion (for LinkedIn)

### Pain Points
- Udemy = cheap but low quality, bloated courses
- Udemy = slow download/playback on slow internet
- Course creators = disappear after purchase (no support)
- Wants confidence instructor is legitimate (not scammer)
- Wants quick access (download & offline access)

### Motivation
- Career growth (skills = promotion + salary increase)
- Passion for learning (intrinsic motivation)
- Peer pressure (colleagues learning, wants to keep up)
- Wants to look smart (certificate = social proof)

### Needs (from LaunchPad / as buyer)
✅ **Professional creator page** (trust = legitimacy)  
✅ **Clear course description** (know what I'm paying for)  
✅ **Author credentials** (why should I trust them?)  
✅ **Testimonials/reviews** (what do other students say?)  
✅ **Fast checkout** (don't make me think)  
✅ **Multiple payment options** (credit card, wallets)  
✅ **Instant download/access** (get it immediately)  
✅ **Refund policy** (safety net if course sucks)  

### Behaviors
- Browses Twitter for learning resources
- Leaves detailed reviews on courses
- Recommends good products to friends
- Price-sensitive but willing to pay for quality
- Purchases monthly (loyal to good instructors)

### Quote
> "I want to buy from someone I trust. A professional landing page with testimonials from other developers reassures me that this course will be worth €50."

## 👥 Engagement Strategy

### For Creators (Pre-Launch)
- Email early adopters (Twitter followers, LinkedIn connections)
- Offer free setup help for first 10 users
- Create video tutorial (5 min quick-start)
- Share success stories (first sales = social proof)

### For Customers
- Word-of-mouth (creators recommend to students)
- Testimonials on landing page (build trust)
- Clear reviews from early buyers
- Professional storefront = legitimacy
